Visiting Us

We love welcoming visitors from other academies. Whether you are in town for a few days or just dropping in for a roll, here is everything you need to know before stepping on the mats.

Drop-In Rates

Casual Visit $25
AET Affiliates $15
1-Week Pass $60

Uniform Policy

  • Gi Classes: Any color Gi is welcome. Please ensure your Gi is washed and smells fresh.
  • No-Gi Classes: Rashguards are required. Shorts must not have pockets or exposed metal zippers.

Arrival

  • Please arrive 10-15 minutes early to sign a waiver and introduce yourself.
  • Footwear must be worn off the mats at all times (bring slides).
