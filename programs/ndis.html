A Tailored Experience

Martial arts is a powerful tool for personal development, but a standard class environment can sometimes be overwhelming. Our NDIS program provides a controlled, 1-on-1 or small group setting where the pace is entirely dictated by the student's needs and comfort levels.

Coaches Will and Angel specialize in adapting techniques to suit different physical and cognitive profiles. Whether the goal is improving gross motor skills, practicing social interaction, or simply finding a healthy outlet for energy, we build a custom training plan for every participant.

Why BJJ for NDIS?

Grappling and striking offer unique therapeutic benefits that traditional sports often lack.

Proprioception & Motor Control

Jiu-Jitsu heavily involves understanding where your body is in space. It is incredible for developing balance, coordination, and fine/gross motor skills in a safe, padded environment.

Routine & Social Skills

The gym provides a structured, predictable routine. Participants learn to interact with coaches, follow multi-step instructions, and practice appropriate social boundaries through training.

Emotional Regulation

Training provides a healthy, heavily supervised outlet for physical energy and frustration. Participants learn breathing techniques and how to stay calm in challenging physical situations.
